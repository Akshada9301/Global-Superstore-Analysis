📊 Global Superstore Business Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

🧩 Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI on the Global Superstore dataset.  

The objective of this project is to solve multiple real-world business problems by transforming raw data into meaningful insights that support decision-making across sales, marketing, finance, and operations.

A total of **30+ business questions** were addressed through interactive dashboards and analytical visuals.

---

🎯 Objectives
- Provide a quick overview of overall business performance
- Analyze sales trends across different time periods
- Understand customer behavior and segmentation
- Identify high-value customers and improve retention strategies
- Evaluate product performance and profitability
- Monitor operational efficiency (order processing & delays)
- Support strategic decision-making using data-driven insights

---

📊 Dashboard Structure

🟢 1. Executive Overview
This page provides a high-level summary of business performance.

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Customers

**Visuals Included:**
- Sales & Profit trend (monthly)
- Yearly sales comparison
- Quarterly sales distribution
- Market-wise performance

**Purpose:**
To enable quick understanding of overall business health.

---

🔵 2. Customer Behavior & Insights
This page focuses on understanding customers and their contribution to the business.

**Analysis Includes:**
- Customer segmentation (Age groups & Income categories)
- Customer churn analysis
- New vs existing customer growth
- High-value customers by region
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

**Purpose:**
To identify valuable customers and improve retention strategies.

---

🟡 3. Sales & Time-Based Analysis
This page explores trends and patterns in sales over time.

**Analysis Includes:**
- Monthly sales trends (2019 focus)
- Financial month & quarter analysis
- Weekend vs weekday sales comparison
- Average yearly sales
- Cumulative sales growth

**Purpose:**
To identify seasonality and sales patterns for better planning.

---

🔴 4. Regional & Performance Analysis
This page evaluates performance across regions and markets.

**Analysis Includes:**
- Sales by region and country
- Top-selling products per region
- Performance gap between leading country and others
- Region-based filtering using profit % parameter
- Top-performing managers by sales

**Purpose:**
To identify high-performing and underperforming regions.

---

🟣 5. Product & Operational Analysis
This page focuses on product performance and operational efficiency.

**Analysis Includes:**
- Top 10 profitable products
- Category & subcategory cost analysis
- Product contribution to profit
- Sales by segment
- Order delay vs on-time delivery analysis

**Purpose:**
To improve product strategy and operational processes.

---

🔍 Key Business Insights
- Sales show strong seasonal patterns, especially in specific quarters  
- A small percentage of customers contributes significantly to total revenue  
- Certain regions consistently outperform others, while some require improvement  
- High-profit products are concentrated within specific categories  
- Delayed orders indicate potential inefficiencies in operations  

---

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI (Data Visualization)
- DAX (Calculations & Measures)
- Data Modeling

---

⚙️ Features Implemented
- Interactive slicers (Segment, Category, Region)
- KPI indicators with targets
- Parameter-based filtering (Profit %)
- Customer segmentation logic (Age & Income bins)
- CLV (Customer Lifetime Value) calculation
- Dynamic and interactive dashboards
